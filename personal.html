 Personality Test Reflection

Particularly in my case i was not pleased with the results. For the reuslts they produced couldn't be further from my actual personality.
They even gave some career suggetions based on my answers they think i would be interested in, or is fitting for me. Not to my surprise, none of the suggetions were remotely close to anything in my field of study's ball park.
